Managable exercises and habits to keep your body fit and healthy

26-Mar-2021 By StayFit Admin

  1. Stay hydrated

    The human body is made up about 60% water, water is essential to the body for many activities, from body temperature control to transportation medium for nutrients and waste products. It is therefore important that we keep ourselves hydrated.

  2. Develop healthy sleeping habits

    Following a sleep schedule can reinforce your body's "sleep-wake" cycle, allowing your body to get into rest time faster, therefore giving you a more energized day.

    Sleeping well also helps one to stay in a better mood, sharpens memory and focus, and helps you learn new things. Studies has shown that in the long term, it also help to lower the risk of a heart disease. Aim to get between 7 to 9 hours of sleep a night.

  3. Be mindful

    Whether its in the form of meditating or simply being one with the nature, being mindful helps to clear your thoughts and let your mind rest. Studies have shown that mindfulness slashes stress, relieves pain and improves your mood.

  4. Eat better food

    Avoid processed food as much as possible.Consume more vegetable sources of protein.

  5. Do simple exercises everyday if you dont have an exercising habits.

    Simply doing exercises such as lunges, jumping jacks, squats, swimming, rope skipping, cycling or even walking around a park can help your muscles to warm up and your body to be more accustomed in delivering oxygen.
