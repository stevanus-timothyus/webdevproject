Diet that keeps you energized throughout your day

26-Mar-2021 By StayFit Admin

While many people sought for fat burning diets, many overlooked the fact that diets and food types can provide more energy to us without making us gain weight. Here are some tips:

  1. Eat small but frequent meals

    Eating small but frequent meals can reduce our perception of fatigue because out brain, which has very few energy reserves of its own, needs a steady supply of nutrients.

  2. Eat food that keeps you fuller for longer period of time

    Starchy carbohydrates such as potatoes, bread, cereals, pasta and rice can help sustain energy and wholegrain or wholemeal varieties will keep you fuller for longer.

  3. Less sugar consumption

    While sugar gives a rush of energy, it can wear off quickly, therefore we should cut down on foot with lots of added sugar such as sweets, cakes, biscuits, sugar fizzy drinks

  4. Eat eggs for breakfast

    Eggs are full of energy that can help fuel your day, they are packed with protein which can give a steady and sustained source of energy.

    Drinking tea and coffee in the morning would give the body caffeine, a stimulant that can increase your energy level. It helps to increase alterness.
